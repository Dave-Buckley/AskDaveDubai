The Views is one of those communities that experienced Dubai residents know about but newcomers often overlook. Tucked between Emirates Golf Club and the Montgomerie Golf Club, it offers something increasingly rare in New Dubai - mid-rise living with genuine green views at a price point well below the neighbouring Marina towers. The apartments here were built in the mid-2000s by Emaar, and the construction quality reflects that era's standards, which were solid.

The community is made up of a series of low- and mid-rise buildings arranged around landscaped courtyards and pools. The architecture is not flashy, but the layouts are practical and the green spaces are well-maintained. Many units have unobstructed views over the Emirates Golf Club fairways - a panorama that would cost considerably more in any of the adjacent communities. One- and two-bedroom apartments dominate the stock, making it particularly attractive to working professionals and couples.

Location is the real story here. The Views is walkable to Dubai Marina, Jumeirah Lakes Towers, and the Media City and Internet City free zones. Dubai Marina Mall and JBR Beach are a short drive or a 15-minute walk away. Sheikh Zayed Road access is direct, and the Metro and Tram network is close enough to use without relying entirely on a car. For someone who works in the Marina corridor and wants to avoid both the premium prices and the high-rise density, this community hits a practical sweet spot.

For investors, The Views delivers reliably. Rental demand stays consistent because the location and price point attract a deep pool of tenants - young professionals, couples, and small families who want a quality address without the premium attached to newer towers. Yields in the 5-7% range are achievable, and the relatively low entry price keeps the investment accessible. The main consideration is building age - some blocks are approaching 20 years old, so buyers should factor in maintenance and check the condition of specific units carefully.

## Where to Eat

The Views has limited dining within the community itself, but its central location means a wide selection of restaurants is only minutes away.

**Within and Adjacent**
- The Links (Emirates Golf Club) - International. Fine dining overlooking the golf course, a short walk from The Views.
- Legends (Emirates Golf Club) - Casual. Relaxed sports bar and grill with outdoor terrace.
- Barista (The Greens) - Cafe. A nearby neighbourhood spot for morning coffee and brunch, 3-minute drive.

**Nearby Options**
- Pier 7 (Dubai Marina) - Multiple restaurants. A full building of dining options across several floors, 8-minute drive.
- JBR The Walk (JBR) - Mixed. Dozens of casual and upmarket restaurants along the beachfront strip, 10-minute drive.

Dubai Marina Mall and its food outlets are a 10-minute walk or a 5-minute drive. For everyday needs, The Greens community retail is adjacent.
