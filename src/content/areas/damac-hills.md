DAMAC Hills is the flagship master-planned community from DAMAC Properties, one of Dubai's largest private developers. Centred around the Trump International Golf Club Dubai, the development spans a substantial footprint south of Al Barsha and offers a genuine alternative to the Emaar-dominated family community market. The mix of property types - from compact apartments to expansive six-bedroom villas - gives it a breadth that appeals to a wide range of buyers and tenants.

The community is organised into themed sub-communities, each with its own architectural character. Golf-facing villas in clusters like Golf Vita, Golf Terrace, and The Field command premium prices, while apartment buildings like Carson and Loreto offer more accessible entry points. Townhouse sub-communities such as Rockwood and Topanga provide a middle ground that has proven particularly popular with young families upgrading from apartment living.

One of the practical advantages of DAMAC Hills is the growing on-site infrastructure. The DAMAC Mall provides everyday retail and dining, and community amenities including swimming pools, gyms, and landscaped parks are well-maintained. Schools in the surrounding area - including GEMS Metropole and South View School - are accessible within a short drive, and the community's position along Umm Suqeim Road provides reasonable connectivity to both the Marina corridor and the Downtown/DIFC axis.

For investors, the value proposition is clear: golf-community living at price points that sit meaningfully below comparable Emaar products. Average prices of AED 1,000-1,600 per square foot represent a discount to Dubai Hills Estate, while rental yields of 5-7% are competitive. As the community continues to mature and the remaining phases complete, DAMAC Hills is positioning itself as one of the more compelling suburban options in New Dubai.

## Where to Eat

The dining scene at DAMAC Hills is still developing but has grown significantly as the community matures. The Trump International Golf Club provides the main sit-down option, with community retail filling in the everyday gaps.

**Within the Community**
- TBAR (Trump International Golf Club) - International. Smart-casual clubhouse dining with views over the course.
- Cafe Bateel (DAMAC Hills Mall) - Cafe. Premium coffee and light bites, popular for working lunches.
- Jones the Grocer (DAMAC Hills community retail) - Cafe/deli. Good for casual breakfasts and weekend brunch.

**Nearby Options**
- Biker's Cafe (Motor City) - International. A laid-back brunch favourite, 10-minute drive.
- The Hamptons Cafe (Al Barsha South) - Cafe. Family-friendly spot, 10-minute drive.

DAMAC Hills Mall continues to add new food and beverage outlets. For a broader selection, Mall of the Emirates is approximately 15 minutes by car.
