Emirates Hills is the pinnacle of villa living in Dubai. Developed by Emaar as one of the earliest master-planned luxury communities, it was designed from the outset to attract ultra-high-net-worth residents who demanded the kind of privacy, space, and exclusivity that high-rise Dubai simply cannot offer. The community wraps around the Emirates Golf Club and the Montgomerie Golf Course, giving many plots direct fairway views or serene lake frontage.

What sets Emirates Hills apart from every other villa community in the city is the sheer scale of its homes. These are not cookie-cutter villas - they are individually designed mansions built on plots that can exceed 30,000 square feet. Owners commission their own architects, and the results range from contemporary minimalism to grand Mediterranean estates. The community is gated with 24/7 security, and traffic within the streets is virtually non-existent.

From a lifestyle perspective, this is old-money Dubai. There are no retail strips, no restaurants within the gates, and no tourist traffic. Residents drive to nearby Dubai Marina or Jumeirah for dining and entertainment, and most have household staff managing day-to-day logistics. The Emirates Golf Club itself is a social hub for residents - membership is highly sought after.

For investors, Emirates Hills is not about rental income. Gross yields sit around 2-3%, which is among the lowest in Dubai. But the value proposition is different here: it is about owning an irreplaceable asset in a supply-constrained community that will never be expanded. Villas that traded for AED 20M a decade ago have appreciated significantly, and the buyer pool - while small - is consistently active at the top end of the market.

## Where to Eat

There are no restaurants within Emirates Hills - it is a fully gated residential community with no commercial outlets. Dining options are found at the adjacent Emirates Golf Club, the nearby Emirates Living retail areas, and the restaurants of Dubai Marina and JBR, all within a short drive.

**Within Emirates Golf Club**
- Montgomerie Clubhouse (Montgomerie Golf Course) - Fine dining and casual terrace overlooking the fairways. Available to members and guests.
- Hive by Emirates Golf Club - Casual dining and lounge in the main clubhouse. Popular for Friday brunch.
- Nineteen (Emirates Golf Club) - Sports bar and restaurant with views over the 9th hole.

**Nearby (5-10 min drive)**
- Dubai Marina and JBR provide hundreds of restaurant options, from waterfront fine dining to casual beachside cafes.
- The Meadows and Springs have small retail strips with cafes, bakeries, and casual dining options convenient for quick errands.
