The Armani Residences occupy floors 9 to 16 of Burj Khalifa and are the only residences in the world designed entirely by Giorgio Armani. Every detail from the flooring to the door handles carries Armani's signature style.

Residents have access to the Armani Hotel's facilities including the spa, swimming pool, restaurants, and a dedicated concierge service. The private lobby and entrance are separate from the hotel and Burj Khalifa observation deck visitors.

These are among the most exclusive addresses in Dubai. Units rarely come to market. Get in touch if you are interested.
