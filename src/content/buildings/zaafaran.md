Zaafaran is an Old Town cluster known for offering some of the best Burj Khalifa views in the community. The low-rise buildings follow the same Arabian architectural style as the rest of Old Town, with units ranging from one to three bedrooms.

The cluster is positioned close to Souk Al Bahar, giving residents easy access to its restaurants, cafes, and waterfront promenade. Like all Old Town buildings, the community is managed by Emaar with shared pools, gardens, and security.
