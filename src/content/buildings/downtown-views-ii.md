Downtown Views II is the kind of building that does not make headlines but consistently delivers for both tenants and investors. Two mid-rise towers sitting on the southern side of Downtown, offering straightforward Emaar-quality apartments at prices that are more accessible than the headline buildings closer to the fountain.

## The Building

Completed in 2014, the twin towers sit between the Boulevard and the southern edge of Downtown, giving residents easy access to both the main Downtown attractions and the wider Business Bay area. The towers are 38 floors each, which is modest by Downtown standards, but the layouts are practical and the building is well maintained by Emaar's community management team.

The lobby and common areas are finished to a decent standard without being flashy. There is no pretence here. This is a solid, functional residential building that does exactly what it needs to do.

## Unit Layouts

One-bedroom units typically range from 700 to 850 square feet. They are compact but efficient, with open-plan living areas and decent kitchen sizes. Two-bedroom units offer more breathing room at 1,100 to 1,400 square feet, with separated bedrooms and a functional living space. Three-bedroom units are available in smaller numbers and cater to families who want Downtown living without paying top-tier prices.

The fit-out is standard Emaar quality. Clean lines, neutral finishes, and practical layouts that have aged well for a 2014 handover.

## Who It Suits

First-time investors looking for a Downtown entry point with strong yields. Young professionals who want to live in Downtown without overstretching their budget. Anyone who prioritises low running costs over hotel-branded amenities.
