Mada Residences is a 57-floor tower developed by ARTAR Real Estate, sitting on the western edge of Downtown Dubai. It does not carry the Emaar badge, but it offers a genuine Downtown address at a lower price point than most of its neighbours, which makes it interesting for both investors and budget-conscious end users.

## The Building

At 57 floors, Mada Residences is one of the taller non-Emaar buildings in Downtown. The design is modern and clean, and the tower holds its own visually alongside its Emaar neighbours. The lobby is well maintained, and the common areas are kept to a reasonable standard.

The location puts you within walking distance of the Boulevard and Dubai Mall, though you are on the western fringe of Downtown rather than right in the middle of it. For some people, that is actually a positive. You get slightly more breathing room, less foot traffic outside your door, and still have everything Downtown offers within a short walk.

## Unit Layouts

The building offers a good range from one-bedroom through to four-bedroom apartments. One-bedroom units start around 750 square feet, and the larger three and four-bedroom units are genuinely spacious. The fit-out is solid without being exceptional. Kitchens are functional, bathrooms are well equipped, and the floor-to-ceiling windows make the most of the views available.

Upper floors facing east get Burj Khalifa and fountain views. North-facing units on higher floors look towards Sheikh Zayed Road and the DIFC skyline.

## Who It Suits

Investors seeking higher net yields within Downtown. Budget-conscious buyers who want a Downtown address without the Emaar price tag. Families who need a larger apartment at a more accessible price point.
