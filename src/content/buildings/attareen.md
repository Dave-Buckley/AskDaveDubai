Attareen is an Old Town cluster that features ground-floor retail and dining, making it one of the more lively parts of the community. The buildings are four to six floors with one to three-bedroom apartments above the commercial units.

The central Old Town location means Souk Al Bahar, the fountain promenade, and Dubai Mall are all a short walk away. The ground-floor restaurants and cafes add convenience for residents.
