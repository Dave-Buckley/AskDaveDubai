Boulevard Plaza is a twin-tower development by Emaar on Mohammed Bin Rashid Boulevard. The towers sit above a retail podium with cafes, restaurants, and shops at ground level, making it one of the more lively addresses on the Boulevard.

Built in 2010, the towers have a good mix of owner-occupiers and tenants. Units range from one to three bedrooms. The location is central to everything in Downtown with Dubai Mall, the fountain promenade, and Burj Khalifa all within walking distance.
