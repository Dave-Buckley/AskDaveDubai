Yansoon is one of the larger clusters within the Old Town community, featuring multiple low-rise buildings arranged around landscaped courtyards. The Arabian-inspired architecture and walkable layout give it a distinctly different feel from the surrounding towers.

Select units offer views of Burj Khalifa, and the community is a short walk from both Souk Al Bahar and Dubai Mall. Yansoon suits residents who prefer a quieter, more grounded living experience while still being in the heart of Downtown.
