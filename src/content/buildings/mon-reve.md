Mon Reve is one of the newest additions to Downtown Dubai, handed over by Emaar in 2024. A boutique 33-floor tower in the Opera District, it brings fresh design and modern finishes to one of the most sought-after pockets of Downtown.

## The Building

At 33 floors and 280 units, Mon Reve is smaller in scale than many of its Downtown neighbours, which gives it a more intimate feel. The building sits in the Opera District, within easy walking distance of Dubai Opera, the Boulevard, and a growing collection of excellent restaurants including Jamavar and MiMi Mei Fair at the nearby Address Residences.

Being a 2024 handover, everything is new. The common areas are contemporary and well finished, the lifts are fast, and the amenity spaces feel current. Emaar's community management handles the day-to-day running.

## Unit Layouts

The unit mix covers one, two, and three-bedroom apartments. Layouts are modern and efficient, making good use of the available space. Kitchens are well equipped with integrated appliances, bathrooms feature quality fixtures, and the overall fit-out reflects Emaar's latest design standards, which are a noticeable step up from their older Downtown buildings.

Floor-to-ceiling windows are standard, and many units benefit from views of the Opera District, the Boulevard, and partial Burj Khalifa sightlines from higher floors.

## Who It Suits

End users who want a brand new Emaar apartment in a premium location. Couples and professionals who value the Opera District lifestyle. Investors targeting tenants who prefer newer buildings with modern finishes.
