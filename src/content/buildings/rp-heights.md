RP Heights is a 32-floor residential tower by RP Group on Mohammed Bin Rashid Boulevard. It is one of the more affordable entry points into Downtown Dubai, with studios and one-bedroom apartments attracting first-time buyers and investors.

Upper floors benefit from partial Burj Khalifa views. The building is within walking distance of the Boulevard's retail and dining strip, and Dubai Mall is a short walk away.
