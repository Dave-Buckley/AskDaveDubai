Reehan is one of the larger Old Town clusters, with multiple buildings ranging from four to seven floors. It sits on the edge of Old Town closest to Mohammed Bin Rashid Boulevard, giving residents direct access to the Boulevard's retail and dining strip.

Units range from studios to three-bedroom apartments. Like all Old Town developments, Reehan features traditional Arabian architecture, shared pools, and landscaped courtyards managed by Emaar community management.
