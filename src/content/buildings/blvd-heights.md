## Overview

BLVD Heights is a twin-tower residential development by Emaar, completed in 2018. The two towers stand at 53 and 47 floors and sit on Mohammed Bin Rashid Boulevard - the main tree-lined road that loops around Downtown Dubai. The Boulevard frontage is a genuine advantage. You are on the main artery of Downtown, within walking distance of the Dubai Fountain, Souk Al Bahar, and Dubai Mall.

The positioning puts BLVD Heights in a sweet spot. You get Burj Khalifa views from Boulevard-facing units, easy access to the restaurants and retail along the Boulevard, and a short walk to the fountain area - without being right on top of the busiest tourist zones. It is Downtown living as it should be: connected but not overwhelmed.

## Units and Layouts

The 480 units across both towers range from one-bedroom to four-bedroom apartments. The mix is weighted toward one and two-bedroom units, but there is a meaningful selection of three and four-bedroom apartments for families. Layouts are modern and open-plan, reflecting Emaar's improved design approach from the late 2010s.

Two-bedroom units are the highlight of the building's inventory. They offer good proportions with well-sized bedrooms, reasonable storage, and living areas that feel spacious without being wasteful. The kitchens are integrated with the living space, which works well for the typical buyer profile here.

Boulevard-facing units in Tower 1 get the Burj Khalifa views and command a premium. Units facing away have views over the wider Downtown district and toward Business Bay - still pleasant, but not the postcard shot. As with all twin-tower developments, spend time understanding which tower and which floor gives you the view and layout combination you want before committing.

## Who It Suits

BLVD Heights works for a wide range of buyers. First-time Downtown buyers looking for a modern apartment at a reasonable price will find a lot to like. Families who want three or four bedrooms on the Boulevard without paying AED 4 million-plus have limited options, and this is one of the best. Investors get solid rental demand and manageable service charges.

It is not the right fit if you want ultra-premium finishes or the lowest possible price in Downtown. For premium, look at Opera Grand or Forte. For budget, look at The Residences. BLVD Heights sits right in the middle - and for many buyers, the middle is exactly where they want to be.
