Burj Crown is a mid-rise residential tower by Emaar, positioned close to Burj Khalifa. At 25 floors it is smaller than many of its neighbours, giving it a more boutique feel with fewer residents sharing the amenities.

Units include one, two, and three-bedroom layouts with modern Emaar finishes. The location is excellent for anyone wanting to be within easy reach of Dubai Mall and the fountain promenade without living in a mega tower.
