48 Burj Gate is a 51-floor tower developed by Enshaa, sitting right on the Boulevard in Downtown Dubai. What makes it notable is that it is one of the few buildings in the district that offers studio apartments, giving buyers and tenants a genuine Downtown entry point at a price that does not require a second mortgage.

## The Building

Completed in 2014, 48 Burj Gate has aged reasonably well. The building is well positioned on Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Boulevard, which means you are within comfortable walking distance of Dubai Mall, the Burj Khalifa, and the fountain shows. The lobby is modest compared to some of the Emaar buildings, but it is clean and functional.

At 51 floors, the building offers solid height, and units from the mid-twenties upward get increasingly good views. The pool and gym facilities are on the podium level and are adequate for a building of this size.

## Unit Layouts

The studio apartments are the headline here. At around 400 to 500 square feet, they are compact but functional, and they represent the most affordable way to own or rent in Downtown Dubai. One-bedroom units range from 700 to 900 square feet, and two and three-bedroom options are available for those wanting more space.

The fit-out is reasonable for the price point. Do not expect the same finish as an Emaar building, but the essentials are covered. Kitchens are functional, bathrooms are well enough equipped, and the floor-to-ceiling windows are standard throughout.

## Who It Suits

Investors looking for the highest yield play in Downtown. First-time buyers wanting to get a foot on the Downtown property ladder. Young professionals who want to live in the district without paying premium rents.
