Miska is a compact cluster of five low-rise buildings in the Old Town community, close to Souk Al Bahar. With around 160 units, it is one of the smaller Old Town developments, which gives it a more intimate community feel.

Units are primarily studios and one to two-bedroom apartments. The location is excellent for the fountain promenade, Souk Al Bahar restaurants, and Dubai Mall.
