Kamoon is part of the Old Town community in Downtown Dubai, a cluster of low-rise buildings with traditional Arabian architecture. The buildings are four to six floors and sit adjacent to Souk Al Bahar, with many units offering direct views of the Dubai Fountain and Burj Khalifa.

Old Town offers a very different lifestyle to the high-rise towers. It is quieter, more walkable, and has a neighbourhood feel with its own cafes, restaurants, and communal areas. Units are smaller and service charges tend to be higher than average due to the extensive community management.
