Address Dubai Opera sits in the heart of the Opera District, one of Downtown Dubai's most sought-after pockets. Two towers connected by a podium, with residences starting from the upper floors for unobstructed views of the Dubai Fountain and Burj Khalifa.

As with all Address-branded residences, owners have access to full hotel services including concierge, housekeeping, valet parking, and the hotel's dining and leisure facilities. The building is within walking distance of Dubai Opera, the Boulevard, and Dubai Mall.
