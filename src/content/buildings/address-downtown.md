## Overview

Address Downtown is where the Address brand started. Completed in 2008, this 63-storey tower sits right on the Dubai Fountain and has become one of the most photographed buildings in the city. If you have ever seen a postcard of Downtown Dubai, chances are this building is in it.

The tower made headlines on New Year's Eve 2015 when a fire broke out across the exterior cladding. Emaar undertook a full renovation and the building reopened in 2020 with upgraded interiors, new cladding, and refreshed common areas. The result is a building that looks and feels far newer than its original 2008 completion date.

## Units and Layouts

There are roughly 220 residential units across the tower, ranging from one-bedroom apartments up to four-bedroom penthouses. Layouts are generous by Downtown standards, particularly the two and three-bedroom units on higher floors. Ceiling heights are good and the floor-to-ceiling windows make the most of the views.

The standout units are the ones facing the Dubai Fountain and Burj Khalifa. These command a significant premium but genuinely deliver one of the best residential views in the city. Units facing other directions are more affordable but obviously lack that wow factor.

## Who It Suits

This building works best for buyers who want a trophy asset in Downtown Dubai, investors targeting the short-term rental market, or end-users who genuinely want hotel-level living. It is not the right fit if you are purely chasing yield on a budget or if you prefer a quieter residential environment.
