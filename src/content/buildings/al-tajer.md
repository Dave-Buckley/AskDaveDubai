Al Tajer is one of the quieter clusters in the Old Town community, with fewer units and a more secluded feel. The low-rise buildings feature the same Arabian architecture as the rest of Old Town, with one and two-bedroom apartments arranged around shared courtyards.

The community is within walking distance of Souk Al Bahar, Dubai Mall, and the fountain promenade, but the cluster itself is set back from the busier areas, making it a good option for residents who want peace and quiet in a central location.
