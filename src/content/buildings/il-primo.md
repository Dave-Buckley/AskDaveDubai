IL Primo is the collection of ultra-premium residences occupying the upper floors of Burj Khalifa, from floor 111 to floor 154. These are marketed separately from the Burj Khalifa Residences (floors 19-108) and represent some of the most exclusive real estate in the world.

Units are large three to five-bedroom apartments and penthouses with panoramic views from over 400 metres above sea level. Residents have a private lobby, dedicated high-speed elevators, and bespoke concierge services. Transactions here are rare and typically in the tens of millions of dirhams.
