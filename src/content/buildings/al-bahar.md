Al Bahar is an Old Town cluster positioned to overlook the Burj Khalifa lake, the body of water from which the Dubai Fountain performs. Select units enjoy some of the most scenic views in the Old Town community.

The buildings follow the same low-rise Arabian architecture as the rest of Old Town, with one to three-bedroom apartments. The cluster is adjacent to Souk Al Bahar, making it one of the most conveniently located Old Town developments.
