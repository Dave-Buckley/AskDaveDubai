## Overview

Vida Residences Downtown is the bigger sibling to Vida Downtown. Completed in 2019, this 56-storey tower sits on Mohammed Bin Rashid Boulevard, right in the heart of Downtown Dubai. At 280 units, it is significantly larger than the original Vida (175 units) and offers a wider range of unit types including three-bedroom apartments.

The building brings the Vida lifestyle approach - design-forward, younger demographic, co-working spaces - to a more prominent Boulevard location. It is closer to the action than Vida Downtown, with direct Boulevard frontage and easier access to Dubai Mall and the Fountain.

One notable feature is the co-working lounge in the lobby. It reflects the Vida brand's understanding that many residents and guests work remotely and want a professional space outside their apartment. It is a small detail but a useful one.

## Units and Layouts

The range runs from studios through to three-bedroom apartments, giving this building a broader appeal than its smaller sibling. Studios are compact at around 400-500 square feet, designed for efficiency rather than space. One-bedroom units (700-900 square feet) are the most popular with investors. Two and three-bedroom units cater to families and those wanting more living space.

Interiors follow the Vida aesthetic - contemporary, clean, and lighter in tone than Address properties. The finishes are good quality without being overly luxurious. It feels modern and fresh, which photographs well for rental listings.

Boulevard-facing units benefit from the street-level energy below and partial views toward the Burj Khalifa. Units on higher floors with Burj Khalifa views command a noticeable premium.

## Who It Suits

Vida Residences Downtown suits investors looking for hotel-branded returns at a more accessible price point than Address. It works well for buy-to-let strategies, particularly studios and one-beds targeting the short-term rental market. End-users who want Boulevard living with lifestyle amenities and a younger vibe will also feel at home here.
