Address Dubai Mall is exactly what it sounds like: an Address Hotels branded residence tower connected directly to the Dubai Mall. For convenience, it is genuinely hard to beat. You can walk from your apartment to 1,200 shops, a cinema, an ice rink, and the largest aquarium in the Middle East without stepping outside.

## The Building

At 68 floors, Address Dubai Mall commands an imposing position on the northern edge of Downtown Dubai. The tower sits right at the junction where the Mall meets the wider district, giving it an unusual advantage. Walk one direction and you are in the Mall. Walk the other and you are on the Boulevard. The Dubai Fountain shows are visible from many of the upper floor units.

The residential component occupies the upper floors, with the hotel and its facilities below. As a branded residence, you get access to all Address Hotels amenities, including restaurants, the spa, the rooftop pool, and concierge services. It is a genuine full-service living experience.

## Unit Layouts

The residential mix is relatively compact compared to some Downtown buildings, with around 200 units in total. One-bedroom units are popular with investors, typically around 800 to 1,000 square feet. Two and three-bedroom units offer more space and better views, with the larger units featuring separate living and dining areas.

The interiors are finished to Address Hotels standards, which means quality fixtures, neutral colour palettes, and the kind of attention to detail you would expect from a five-star hotel brand.

## Who It Suits

Short-term rental investors who want premium nightly rates and high occupancy. End users who prioritise convenience and hotel-level services above all else. Buyers who want a turnkey, fully serviced residence without managing anything themselves.
