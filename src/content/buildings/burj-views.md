## Overview

Burj Views is a three-tower residential complex by Emaar, completed in 2009. Towers A and C rise to 38 floors while Tower B stands at 34 floors. The complex sits on a shared podium with retail space at ground level.

The towers offer studios, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom apartments. This is one of the more affordable entry points into Downtown Dubai, making it popular with investors looking for rental yield and young professionals who want the Downtown lifestyle without the premium price tag.

## Location

Burj Views sits alongside Burj Park and the Burj Khalifa lake, giving many units direct views of the Dubai Fountain show. Dubai Mall is a short walk away, and the Boulevard is on your doorstep.

The complex benefits from its position near the centre of Downtown without the higher price per square foot commanded by towers directly on the Boulevard or overlooking the Opera District.

## Summary

Burj Views is a solid value play in Downtown Dubai. The towers are older (2009) so finishes are not as modern as newer builds, but the location is excellent and the lower price per square foot means better rental yields compared to premium towers.

Studios and one-beds here rent quickly. If you are buying to let, this is one of the smarter picks in Downtown. If you are buying to live and want modern finishes, you may want to budget for a renovation.
