Zanzebeel is one of the original clusters in the Old Town community, built in 2008 as part of Emaar's vision for a low-rise, Arabian-style neighbourhood within Downtown Dubai. The buildings are four to six floors with shared courtyards, pools, and landscaped gardens.

Units range from one to three bedrooms. The location provides easy access to the Boulevard, Souk Al Bahar, and Dubai Mall while maintaining the quieter pace of Old Town living.
