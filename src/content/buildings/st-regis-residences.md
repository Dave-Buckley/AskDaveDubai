The St. Regis Residences are a pair of towers on Mohammed Bin Rashid Boulevard, managed by St. Regis (part of the Marriott group). Residents benefit from the hotel's signature butler service, along with access to the pool, fitness centre, and dining.

The towers offer a mix of layouts from one-bedroom apartments to four-bedroom penthouses, many with direct Burj Khalifa or Boulevard views. The location puts you within walking distance of Dubai Mall and the Downtown promenade.
