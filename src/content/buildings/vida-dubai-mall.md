Vida Dubai Mall is a twin-tower hotel-residence development with a direct pedestrian connection to Dubai Mall. The Vida brand targets a younger, lifestyle-focused audience compared to the more traditional Address properties.

Units are compact and well-designed, ranging from studios to two-bedroom apartments. The building includes co-working spaces, a rooftop pool, social lounges, and the signature Vida dining concepts. Popular with investors for its short-term rental performance given the Dubai Mall footfall.
