## Overview

Vida Downtown is the original Vida property and it set the tone for what the brand represents - lifestyle-focused, design-driven, and pitched at a younger demographic than the more formal Address brand. Completed in 2018, this 35-storey tower sits in the Opera District area of Downtown Dubai, within walking distance of Dubai Opera and the Boulevard.

With only 175 residential units, this is a boutique building by Downtown standards. The lower unit count is deliberate - Vida positions itself as a curated lifestyle experience rather than a mass-market hotel. That smaller scale shows in the quieter lobby, the less crowded pool, and the more personal feel of the building.

The ground-floor restaurant, La Serre, is a well-known French bistro that adds genuine character to the building. It is one of those dining spots that locals actually go to, not just hotel guests.

## Units and Layouts

Units range from studios to two-bedroom apartments. Studios are compact but well-designed, typically around 400-500 square feet with clever storage solutions and a modern aesthetic. One-bedroom units run 650-850 square feet and offer a comfortable living space for singles or couples.

The interiors lean toward a contemporary, minimalist design with lighter tones than the Address properties. Think clean whites, natural wood accents, and modern fixtures. It feels younger and more Instagram-friendly, which is exactly what the brand is going for.

Views vary by floor and position. Higher floors offer decent Burj Khalifa angles, while lower units look out over the surrounding low-rise Opera District or toward Sheikh Zayed Road.

## Who It Suits

Vida Downtown is ideal for buyers who value design and atmosphere over scale and views. It suits younger investors, owner-occupiers who want a boutique hotel experience, and buyers who prefer the quieter Opera District side of Downtown over the busier Mall area.
