## Overview

Address Fountain Views is the largest Address complex in Downtown Dubai. Three towers - 65, 55, and 50 storeys - sit on a shared podium directly adjacent to Dubai Mall and the Dubai Fountain. Completed in 2020, it was purpose-built to capitalise on the fountain views that make Downtown Dubai famous.

The development is split between hotel keys and residential units, with approximately 800 residential apartments across the three towers. The sheer scale of this project sets it apart from the more boutique Address Downtown. You are getting the same brand and service level, but in a much larger setting.

## Units and Layouts

Units range from one-bedroom apartments to three-bedroom units. There are no studios here, which pushes the entry price higher than Address Boulevard. The one-bedroom layouts typically start around 750 square feet and are well-proportioned with modern Address interiors.

The key differentiator is the views. Units in Tower 1 (the tallest at 65 floors) with direct fountain-facing positions are the most desirable. Towers 2 and 3 offer varying perspectives - some have excellent Burj Khalifa angles, while others face out toward the sea or Sheikh Zayed Road. Not every unit in this complex has a fountain view, despite the name. Make sure you check exactly what your unit looks out on before buying.

Finishes are consistent across all three towers - neutral palettes, quality fixtures, and the usual Address standard. The podium level includes a large pool deck, gym, and leisure facilities shared across the towers.

## Who It Suits

Address Fountain Views is a strong option for investors who want Address branding and fountain proximity at a lower per-square-foot cost than Address Downtown. It also works for end-users who want to live close to Dubai Mall with hotel services but do not need the prestige of the original Address tower.
